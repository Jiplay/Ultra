Bench Press

Chest Triceps Shoulders
1

Lie flat on the bench with your eyes under the bar. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.

2

Unrack the bar and lower it to your mid-chest while keeping your elbows at a 45° angle.

3

Press the bar back up in a slight arc, locking out at the top without flaring your elbows.

4

Keep your feet flat, glutes on the bench, and maintain a slight arch in your lower back throughout.
